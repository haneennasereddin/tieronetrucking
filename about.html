About Us

We are
Tier One Trucking

Founded in 2021, Tier One Trucking is a new company with fresh ideas and tier one customer service. We understand what it takes to not only keep our customers happy, but our employees as well.
Tier One is a growing transportation company located in Palos Hills, IL. We will strive to provide our employees the best opportunity possible including the ability to work whenever and wherever most convenient. Tier One Trucking will continue to work for you as much as you work for us.
We plan on building on a reputation in which we work 24/7 to provide high quality services to all our clients. Investing in our employees to keep them just as happy as our customers is one of our biggest goals.
